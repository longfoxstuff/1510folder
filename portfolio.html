A portfolio is being used here to document your mastery of the skills, goals, and standards of this class. It also puts together, in one place, most of the work you've done this quarter which can be used for reference at a later time.

The reflection statement is for you to think about (reflect on) your thought processes in putting this project together, the progress of your learning in this course, and what you personally gained from taking the class

The portfolio should be done using Microsoft Word.

The portfolio should include the following items:

  • A table of contents

  • The syllabus

  • The schedule

  • All homework assignments

  • All projects including the final project

  • All quizzes

  • All handouts

  • Any articles for extra credit

  • Any research papers for extra credit

  • The rubric for this assignment

  • A reflection statement on this assignment

The portfolio will be turned in via e-mail during the last week of regular classes.
