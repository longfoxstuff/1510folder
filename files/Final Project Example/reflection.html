Reflection

When I was creating this website I often thought back to my other website Code Warehouse on the many techniques we used to create the forms, headers, and style in general. I wanted a really simplistic layout that almost anyone could pick up and modify to their hearts content and I think I hit the nail on the head with this layout. Basically I feel as if I completed my objectives for the website without over-doing it.

Grade:

Because of the possibilities with the layout I created and everything that I have put on here I give myself a 3 due to the fact that I can easily modify the layout to fit my needs in the future.
